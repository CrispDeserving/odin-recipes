Nasi Goreng

Description

A tasty Indonesian Dish with stir-fried vegetables, prawns, and rice for a flavorful meal.

Link to the original recipe (from AllRecipes.com)

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Eggs
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Toasted Sesame Oil
  • Sweet Chili Sauce
  • Zucchini
  • Carrot
  • Green Onion
  • Cooked Rice
  • Cooked Prawns
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Beat and season the eggs. Cook at the desired doneness.
  2. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, sesame oil, and chili sauce. Set aside.
  3. Heat the skillet with oil over medium-high heat. Add zucchini, carrot, and green onions. Stir fry for 3 minutes. Add the garlic, rice, prawns, and soy sauce mixture, and mix well.
  4. Remove from heat. Top with the scrambled eggs and chives.
