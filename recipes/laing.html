Laing

Description

A good Filipino dish from the region of Bicol. It combines dried taro leaves, coconut milk, pork, and chili peppers. A meaty, smooth dish that everyone will enjoy. Pairs excellently with rice.

Link to the original recipe (from Kawaling Pinoy)

Ingredients (Measurements in the website linked)

  • Dried Taro (or Gabi) Leaves
  • Neutral Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Pork (preferably the belly)
  • Pepper
  • Shrimp Paste
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chili Peppers
  • Coconut Cream
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Shred the taro leaves into pieces into around 3-by-5 inches in size.
  2. In a wide pan, heat on medium heat with oil. Add onions, garlic, and ginger to cook, stirring occasionally until aromatic.
  3. Add pork and cook until golden brown. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add shrimp paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the coconut milk and simmer. Then add the chili peppers.
  5. Add the taro leaves, pushing them into the liquid to moisten. Simmer without stirring for about 20 minutes.
  6. Add the coconut cream and cook for 15 minutes until dry and fat begins to render. Season with salt to taste.
